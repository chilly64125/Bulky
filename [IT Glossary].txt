hashtag "#"
------------------------------------------------------------
A hashtag is a word or phrase preceded by the "#" symbol, 
used on social media to categorize content and make it more discoverable. 
By clicking on a hashtag, users can see other posts with the same tag, 
which helps them find topics or conversations they are interested in. 
For example, using #skincare on a post about a new moisturizer helps 
people searching for "skincare" find your content. 
Function: Hashtags serve as a way to tag and cross-reference content by topic or theme, 
turning them into searchable links.
Usage: You create a hashtag by placing the "#" symbol directly before a keyword 
or phrase without spaces or special characters, such as #autumnleaves.
Purpose: They make it easier for users to find and engage with content relevant 
to their interests. They also act as a signal for algorithms to categorize 
and recommend posts.
Placement: Hashtags can be included anywhere within a post.
History: The symbol "#" is known as a number sign, hash, or pound sign, 
and its use as a hashtag was popularized on platforms like X (formerly Twitter).
